LAAM - THE ARABIC LETTER PART 2

INTRODUCTION

The Arabic letter Laam () is equivalent to the letter 'L' in the English alphabet. The position of the letter Laam () in the normal Arabic character set is 23. In the numerical character set, Abjad, Laam () is the 12th Letter with a numerical value of 30. This article is about the spiritual meaning of the letter Laam ().

 

THE LETTER LAAM PART 2

There is one quality in the Letter Laam () that is not found in any other Arabic Letter. The Letter Laam () when written as a word becomes Laam Alif Meem (). The Letter Laam as a word contains the Letters Alif () and Meem (). No other Arabic Letter when written as a word contains both Alif () and Meem ().

There are only 3 Arabic Letters when written as words (not counting the Letter Laam () itself) which contain the Letter Laam (). These are:

Since the numerical value of the Letter Laam () is 30, it is basically connected with the number 3. Therefore the Letter Laam ( = 30) is connected to the Letter Jeem ( = 3) and also to the Letter Sheen (= 300). That is the reason behind the numerical values in the Abjad format. To show connections between Letters. How is that connected with the Quran ?

ALIF LAAM MEEM

Our Guidance, the Quran starts with these 3 Muqattaat Letters - Alif Laam Meem.

1 Alif Laam Meem
2 That is the Book in which there is no doubt, a guidance to those who ward off (evil).

[Quran: Al Baqara Chapter 2]

The majority of human beings are by nature prone to evil, the exception being the Messengers () of Allah. Evil is one of the characteristics of the human soul. And the Quran has been given to us to rectify that. The Quran also confirms evil characteristic of the human soul as follows:

53 "Nor do I absolve my own soul (of blame): the human soul is certainly prone to evil unless my Rabb (Lord) do bestow His Mercy: but surely certainly my Rabb (Lord) is Forgiving Merciful."

[Quran: Yusuf Chapter 12]

The soul which is prone to evil is called Nafs Ammarah. Nafs Ammarah is a destructive soul. It tries and destroys the person through making the person restless and reckless.

Then there is the other kind of soul which is always 'accusing'. This kind of soul is called Nafs Lawwamah. It whispers Shaytan's (satanic) thoughts. This kind of soul is restless. The person may not carry out the evil suggestions of Shaytan but the soul nevertheless makes the person restless.

2 And I do call to witness the accusing soul

[Quran: Al Qiyamat Chapter 75]

And finally there is a third kind of soul which is peaceful. This kind of soul is called Nafs Mutmainnah. It belongs to the Messengers () of Allah and to the pious people.

27 You the peaceful soul!

[Quran: Al Fajr Chapter 89]


The interesting thing about all this is the fact that Nafs Ammarah the reckless soul can be changed to the Nafs Lawwamah the restless soul. And then the Nafs Lawwamah can be changed to Nafs Mutmainnah the PEACEFUL soul. How ?

By following the Guidance in the Quran. Alif Laam Meem. Meem is the Mutmainnah PEACEFUL soul.

THE FOUR MAIN ANGELS

The four main angels who are connected with the affairs of the earth are :

IsrafeeL, JibraeeL, MikaeeL and AzraeeL. The interesting thing to note here is that their names end with the Letter Laam (). These four angels are in charge of numerous other angels who carry out the necessary tasks on earth under their command.

If we look at the names of the four main angels we find that:

Israfeel ()'s name starts with the Letter Alif ()

Jibraeel ()'s name starts with the Letter Jeem ()

Mikaeel ()'s name starts with the Letter Meem ()

Azraeel ()'s name starts with the Letter Ayn ()

If we add the numerical values of the initial Letters of these 4 angels we get:

Alif ( = 1) + Jeem ( = 3) + Meem ( = 40) + Ayn ( = 70) = 114

That is exactly equal to the number of chapters in the Holy Quran.
