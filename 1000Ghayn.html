GHAYN - THE ARABIC LETTER

INTRODUCTION

The Arabic Letter Ghayn () has no equivalent sounding letter in the English alphabet. The position of the Letter Ghayn () in the normal Arabic character set is 19. In the numerical character set, Abjad, Ghayn () is the 28th and last Letter. The numerical value of the Letter Ghayn () is 1000. This article is about the spiritual meaning of the letter Ghayn ().

 

THE LETTER GHAYN

The Letter Ghayn () is the final letter in the Abjad format. It is the 28th Letter with a value of 1000. That is the highest number that is used for a Letter in the Abjad. One thousand in Arabic is Alif. With the end of the twentieth century, we keep hearing the word Millennium. Millennium is also equal to 1000. Can we say that the Letter Ghayn () has a numerical value equal to 'Millennium' ?

The Letter Ghayn () is for Ghafar - To Pardon, To Forgive.

 

GHAFAR - TO FORGIVE

If we look in the Quran, we find numerous verses where it is mentioned that Allah is Forgiving and Merciful. For example:

28 You that believe! Fear Allah and believe in His messenger. He will give you a double portion of His Mercy: He will provide for you a light by which you shall walk and He will forgive you. Allah is Forgiving. Most Merciful;

[Quran: Al Hadeed Chapter 57]

The most important thing to keep in mind is that Belief in Allah and His Rasool (Messenger) is the basis for forgiveness. We must believe in Allah and His Rasool Muhammad in this world, in order to receive Mercy and be Forgiven in this and the next world. Without belief there is nothing but torment in the next world. And Allah has emphasised this in the Quran:

80 Whether you ask for their forgiveness or not (their sin is unforgivable): if you ask seventy times for their forgiveness Allah will not forgive them: because they rejected Allah and His Rasool; and Allah does not guide wrongdoers.

[Quran: At Tawba Chapter 9]

There is no forgiveness for those who disbelieve.

 

THE GHAUSS

The word Ghauss starts with the Letter Ghayn (). The Ghauss is the spiritual leader of all the Awliya Allah - friends of Allah. There is a Ghauss who is very well known in the Islamic world, his name is Shaikh Abdul Qadir al Jilani (may Allah Shower His Blessings on him). He is the Ghauss al Azam. He is the leader of all the other Ghauss. Those that accept him as their spiritual master are known as Qadiri. Sultan Bahu () - Sultan al Aarifeen who also belonged to the Qadiriya order has stated in his books, that ' Those who follow the Qadiriya Spiritual Order do not go to any other Spiritual Orders. Because the furthest limit of the other orders is only the beginning stage of the Qadiriya Order. '

What is the purpose of Ghauss or for that matter Awliya (friends of) Allah.

62 Behold! verily on the friends of Allah there is no fear nor shall they grieve;
63 Those who believe and (constantly) guard against evil
64 For them are Glad Tidings in the life of the Present and in the Hereafter: no change can there be in the Words of Allah. This is indeed the supreme triumph.

[Quran: Yunus Chapter 10]

The Awliya Allah are the heirs of the Messengers of Allah (). They help others in the Name of Allah, those that need help. They take on the duty of asking Allah for forgiveness for all the Muslims. They guide those whom Allah wants to guide. The early saints have left their teachings in the shape of books. These books are available for us all to learn from. Let us look at one example and learn a lesson.

 

A LESSON

As we race forward in time to the next Millennium (2000 AD), let us go back to the previous Millennium prior to the year 1000 AD and learn a lesson from a very early noble saint.

We shall look at an example that is connected with the Letter Ghayn () from Kashf Al Mahjub by Ali bin Usman al Hujwiri who is also known as Datta Ganj Baksh or simply Datta Sahib (- may Allah Shower His Blessings on him):

There are two veils:

One is the "veil of covering (hijaabi Rayni)" which can never be removed.

The other is the "veil of clouding (hijaabi Ghayni)" which is quickly removed.

The explanation is as follows: one man is veiled from the Truth by his essence (zaat), so that in his view truth and falsehood are the same. Another man is veiled from the Truth by his attributes (sifaat), so that his nature and heart continually seek the Truth and flee from falsehood. Therefore the veil of essence (Rayni) is never removed. Rayn is synonymous with sealing and imprinting. Thus Allah has said:

14 By no means! but on their hearts is the stain of the (ill) which they do!

[Quran: Al Mutaffifin Chapter 83]

... But the veil of attributes, which is that of "clouding (Ghayni)", may be removed at times, for essence does not admit of alteration, but the alteration of the attributes is possible.

[Kashf Al Mahjub Chapter: Introduction]

Datta Sahib is a Ghauss of his time.

 

HIJAABI GHAYNI - VEIL OF GHAYNI

What is Hijaabi Ghayni - Veil of Ghayni ? How do we remove the Veil Hijaabi Ghayni ? We need a teacher who shows us how. Coming back to Rayni and Ghayni, the Letter Ra () for Rayni is without a dot. So it cannot be changed as such. In other words, there is not much that can be 'removed'. Whereas the Letter Ghayn () for the Veil Hijaabi Ghayni, has a dot. The dot can be removed and the Letter Ghayn () becomes the Letter Ayn (). And what is Ayn () ?

Ayn is Eye. The Ayn (eye) is connected with vision which in this context is Aql - Intellect. And that Aql is to be used with Presence - Hudhoor (). Hijaab - Veil is also with the Letter Ha (). By 'Presence' it is meant, absent from the self and Present before Allah. Removing that veil makes the person more alert to the inner meanings of outward requirements of Islam.

May Allah remove the Hijaabi Ghayni from us and enable us to understand Islam as it really should be understood.
