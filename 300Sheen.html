SHEEN - THE ARABIC LETTER

INTRODUCTION

The Arabic letter Sheen () is the 13th letter in the Arabic character set. Sheen () in Arabic numerology which is known as Abjad, has the value of 300. This article is about the spiritual meaning of the letter Sheen ().

 

THE LETTER SHEEN

The Letter Sheen () is used for Shahid - Witness. It is also used for Shaytan.

A Shahid is a witness. The Muslims are a community of witnesses. Why are the Muslims a community of witnesses ?

 

SHAHADAT - TO WITNESS

DEFINITION OF WITNESS

WITNESS (noun) TESTIMONY; Attestation of a FACT or EVENT; One who KNOWS or SEES anything; One who gives evidence in a trial.

In any court of law, in any country, a witness is someone who has evidence of a fact or event. That means the person knows about or has seen the event.

A person who is not present at the event in question, cannot be called a witness. A person who has no connection with the event in question, cannot be called a witness.

To put it another way:

No one will call anyone a witness, a person who is absent from the event in question, and therefore that person has nothing to do with, or has no connection with the event in question.

 

KALIMA TAYYAB

LA ILAHA ILL ALLAHU MUHAMMADUR RASOOL ALLAH

THERE IS NO GOD ONLY ALLAH MUHAMMAD IS THE RASOOL OF ALLAH

This Kalima Tayyab is the minimum requirement for a non-Muslim to repeat with the intention of becoming a Muslim. Allah accepts that person as a Muslim. For a Muslim, the belief in this Kalima is the basic requirement. The second Kalima is progression to a stage that is higher than this stage. The second Kalima is the Shahadah.

 

KALIMA SHAHADAT

ASH HADU AL LA ILAHA ILL ALLAHU WA ASH HADU AN NA MUHAMMADAN ABDUHU WA RASOOLUH

I TESTIFY (BEAR WITNESS TO THE FACT) THERE IS NO GOD ONLY ALLAH AND I TESTIFY MUHAMMAD IS HIS SERVANT AND RASOOL (MESSENGER)

The second Kalima is much more involved. In every two Rakah of Salah (prayer) it is recited once. In every four Rakah Salah it is recited twice. It is recited from 17 to 23 times in total (counting all the Salah) every 24 hours, depending on the number of Rakah of the four different schools.

The Muslims are the only community who are called as WITNESSES to this FACT 17 to 23 times a day. The Muslims are CALLED to TESTIFY this fact. The Muslims are called to give evidence. That is one of the reasons why the Salah (prayer) is the Miraaj (Ascension) of the believers.

37 In this is a Message for any that has a heart and understanding or who gives ear and earnestly witnesses (the truth).

[Quran: Qaf Chapter 50]

The reference here is to those who "give ear". Those who listen to what they themselves are saying. In other words, we must listen to what we recite and understand what we recite.

AND I TESTIFY MUHAMMAD IS THE MESSENGER OF ALLAH

But Shaytan plays tricks on us. And we fail to understand the second testimony.

 

SHAYTAN

Shaytan was a witness in the presence of Allah. He was a witness to the creation of Adam (). Yet he rebelled. Why ?

In the beginning there was Allah. He created the angels before he created Adam (). Iblees or Shaytan was called Azazeel in those days. Azazeel was given superiority over the angels. Azazeel was the teacher of the angels. He had been praising Allah for 70,000 years.

Then Allah created Adam () and He taught him knowledge. Allah commanded the angels to bow down to Adam ().

34 And behold We said to the angels: "Bow down to Adam"; and they bowed down not so Iblees he refused out of pride he was of those who reject Faith.

[Quran: Al Baqara Chapter 2]

They did except Iblees. Why did Iblees not bow down?

75 (Allah) said: "Iblees! what prevents you from prostrating yourself to one whom I have created with My hands? Are you proud? Or are you one of the high (and mighty) ones?"
76 (Iblees) said: "I am better than he: You created me from fire and him You created from clay.

[Quran: Saad Chapter 38]

Iblees was hurt. His pride was crushed. Here he was praising Allah for 70,000 years or more and along came Adam (). Allah commanded Azazeel (70,000+ years old) to bow down to a 'child' (relatively speaking in terms of creation time). The other blow to Iblees was that he is madly in love with Allah, and Allah prefers to love Adam () more, rather than him. Honouring Adam () over Shaytan.

Shaytan lost everything in one moment of pride. Why did he lose it all?

He thought he was superior creation to Adam (). But Adam () had something superior in his creation.

72 And when I have fashioned him and breathed into him of My spirit, then fall down before him prostrate,

[Quran: Saad Chapter 38]

What is the superiority of Adam () ? Allah had breathed into him of His spirit. Allah commanded the angels to bow down to the superiority of Adam(), but Iblees was too engrossed in looking at the physical side of Adam () that he was unaware of the spiritual side of Adam (). Shaytan made the mistake initially and fell from grace. Now he started to look for a way to be forgiven. He waited a long time.

 

SHAYTAN ASKING FOR FORGIVENESS

One day in the time Musa (), Shaytan came to Musa (). Shaytan asked Musa () to ask Allah on his behalf to forgive him (Shaytan). Musa () said he would. He asked Allah to forgive Shaytan. Allah said He would forgive Iblees on the condition that he goes to the grave of Adam () and bows down. When Musa () told Shaytan of the condition for forgiveness, he again became proud, and said, it is because of Adam () I am in this state. Why should I bow down to him now. Shaytan again waited for a very very long time.

Muhammad was sitting in the Mosque one day and Shaytan approached him. Shaytan said to Muhammad

I have a Dua - prayer which when I recite, I can go to heaven and return. If you want, I can teach you that prayer and you too can visit heaven and return every time you recite that prayer.

In the blink of an eye, Jibreel () appeared. Jibreel () said to Muhammad to accept the Dua that Shaytan will tell him, and it is true what he says.

Jibreel () further informed Muhammad that:

"Shaytan has an ulterior motive. He knows you are soft hearted, and you may ask Allah to forgive him for his good deed. If you do ask Allah to forgive Shaytan, Allah will accept your prayer. Therefore accept the Dua that Shaytan gives you and leave his fate in the Hands of Allah. "

So, Muhammad accepted the Dua from Shaytan and said to him that: Allah will reward you as He sees fit.

Shaytan lost. Closer to the Day of Judgement, Shaytan will be made to forget this Dua.

What about the fate of Shaytan on the Day of Judgement ?

 

SHAYTAN'S FATE

It is related in TAZKIRA GHAUSIYA by Sayyid Ghauss Ali Shah Qalandri Pani Patee ():

On the Day of Reckoning Allah will command 70,000 angels to chain Shaytan and take him to hell. On hearing this, Shaytan will fall down on the ground where the Judgement gathering will be taking place. The angels will use their combined strength, but they will not succeed in even shaking him. Then 70,000 more angels will be commanded to join the previous 70,000. 140,000 angels will not be able to move him. Then Allah will command a further 70,000 angels and then again a further 70,000. A total of 280,000 angels will not succeed in moving Shaytan.

Then Allah will tell the angels, "You cannot move him. He has the weight of disgrace around his neck. It is only his courage, that he has been roaming around all this time carrying that weight of disgrace. He cannot be moved by you. He is truly in love with Me. When We command him, he will go and fall in hell by himself. You leave him alone".

May Allah have Mercy on us all. Ameen.
