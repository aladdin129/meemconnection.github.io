BISMILLAH HIR RAHMAN NIR RAHEEM

WHY WERE ALL OF ALLAH'S MESSENGERS MEN ?

It was stated in Meem Part 1....

It is a requirement for Prophethood that the person is not a woman. It must be man. A woman cannot be a prophet. Allah says:

109 And We did send before you (Messengers) all men...

[Quran: Yusuf Chapter 12]

Let us also look at another verse from the Quran:

Noon wal Qalam wa ma Yastaroon

1 Noon and the pen and that which they write

[Quran: Al Qalam Chapter 68]

What does it mean ? ...and that which they write... Who writes ?

Have you ever considered why ?

Did Allah not create Adam (as) before Hawwa (as) ?

Did Allah not create the Qalam first and then the Lawh ?

What is Qalam ?

Qalam is a Pen. What does a pen have in order to write ? It must have ink. The Qalam is associated with the male species.

What is Lawh ?

Lawh is the tablet on which the Qalam - Pen writes. Lawh is associated with the female species. The ink writes on the Lawh. The Qalam deposits ink in the Lawh. WORDS are written on the Lawh. In Kashf Al Mahjub it is stated by Datta Gang Baksh, the "SECRET" is such that it is beyond human reasoning. Reason cannot grasp it. When the secret dawns, reason abdicates the mind. After abdicating, it tries to get a grip on the intelligence. But it fails.

The secret of creation has been placed by Allah in the 'ink drop' that is "EMITTED". Yet if there is a trace of that "ink drop" on the garments, Salah is not acceptable.

That is precisely why for Prophethood, it is a requirement that the person is a man and not a woman. Because the secret of creation has been placed in the sperm drop by Allah. The sperm drop is shaped like the letter Meem (). When Allah created Isa () from Maryam (), Allah created Isa () from a MEEM (). If we write the letter Meem () as a word as it is pronounced Meem Ya Meem , what do we find in the middle of the two Meem letters. We find the letter Ya (). Have you ever considered why Hadrat Usman Ghani () placed the two dots below the letter Ya () and not above ? He () was told to do so.

Keep the human body in mind and consider where the Qalam is and where does the ink come from ? Can a female fulfil this requirement ? NO! Can a man fulfil this requirement ? Yes!

There is a Hadees in which it is stated, that if the Sajdah was permissible for any other than Allah, then it would become necessary on the woman to bow down to her husband. Why ? Because the secret of the creation from Meem () is placed inside a male by Allah.

Now we know what the Qalam is, it emits "white" or "milky" ink. What is the meaning of Noon Wal Qalam -Noon and the Pen ?

There are two Noons () and these also play an important role in the development of a human being or most other mammals for that reason, the Qalam starts the process of creation and the two Noons () complete the infancy when the infant is being suckled. These two forms of ink play a major part in the development of the human being by Allah's command. Think about what has been written above, it is the truth. And here are two Hadees quotations from Sahih Al Bukhari:

Uqba married the daughter of Abu Ihab bin Aziz, and then a woman came and said, "I suckled 'Uqba and his wife." 'Uqba said to her, "I do not know that you have suckled me, and you did not inform me." He then sent someone to the house of Abu Ihab to inquire about that but they did not know that she had suckled their daughter. Then 'Uqba went to the Prophet in Medina and asked him about it. The Prophet said to him, "How (can you keep your wife) after it has been said (that both of you were suckled by the same woman)?" So, he divorced her and she was married to another (husband).

My foster uncle came and asked permission (to enter) but I refused to admit him till I asked Allah's Apostle about that. He said, "He is your uncle, so allow him to come in." I said, "O Allah's Apostle! I have been suckled by a woman and not by a man." Allah's Apostle said, "He is your uncle, so let him enter." And that happened after the order of Al-Hijab (compulsory veiling) was revealed. All things which become unlawful because of blood relations are unlawful because of the corresponding foster suckling relations. [Aisha ()]

There is a Hadees in which it is stated that while burying someone, something fell in the grave. Rasool Allah said, let the person who did not have relations with his wife the night before go down in the grave and remove it. Do you know why ?

Because the grave is like a womb. In the first mother's womb we are created from nothing but a sperm drop. In the second mother's womb ("mother earth") the creation process is reversed and we are reduced to dust. Then we will be created again from this same mother earth's womb on judgement day. And what does the Hadees tell us about the second creation ? It will rain, and the rain will be like semen.
